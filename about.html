About ESSENCE

Discover the story behind our passion for perfumery

Our Story

Founded in 2010, ESSENCE began as a small boutique in Paris with a simple mission: to create exceptional fragrances that tell a story and evoke emotions. Our founder, Sophie Laurent, a third-generation perfumer, combined traditional French perfumery techniques with modern innovation to create scents that are both timeless and contemporary.

What started as a passion project has grown into a global brand, but our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and sustainability remains unchanged. Each fragrance is still carefully crafted in our original atelier in Grasse, the perfume capital of the world.

Craftsmanship

We believe that creating a fine fragrance is an art form. Our perfumers spend years mastering their craft, training their noses to identify thousands of scents and understand how they interact with each other and with the skin.

Each ESSENCE fragrance is the result of hundreds of iterations, meticulous attention to detail, and a deep understanding of the emotional power of scent.

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. Our ingredients are ethically sourced from sustainable farms around the world, and we work directly with local communities to ensure fair trade practices.

Our packaging is made from recycled materials and is fully recyclable. We are constantly innovating to reduce waste and improve our sustainability practices.

Our Team

Behind every ESSENCE fragrance is a team of passionate individuals dedicated to creating exceptional scents. From our master perfumers to our customer service representatives, everyone plays a vital role in bringing our vision to life.
